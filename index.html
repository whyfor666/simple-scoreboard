Simple Scoreboard
Quick Tutorial

  • Tap team name to change it
  • Tap team logo to begin scoring
  • Tap team score to increase it
  • Long-press score to decrease it
  • Long-press from 0 displays logo

  • Mouse Left-click = Tap
  • Mouse Right-click = Long-press
  • HR Animation keys: 0–3

(Tip: Hold [⚙Info?] to go directly to settings)
